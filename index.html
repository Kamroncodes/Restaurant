Our Story

Tachatan and his brother Nichnop grew up in Saraburi near the famous Kao Yai. They grew up working on their family's flower farm where they learned how to cultivate healthy plants. They decided to expand their farm and started growing fruits and vegetables. Then using their grandma's recipes and techniques, the brothers learned how to use their fresh grown produce and create healthy delicious fresh dishes to be served and shared.
